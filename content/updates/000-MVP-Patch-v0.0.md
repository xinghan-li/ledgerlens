**March 5, 2026** — Today we’re bringing LedgerLens online.

In a time when AI capabilities are evolving rapidly, many of our ideas—and the tasks that used to feel tedious—are changing. The equilibrium among time, cost, and value has been reshaped. I never thought I’d be able to build an app like this on my own in the pre–AI era. But here it is.

This release is far from perfect; it’s not even close to the 10% I have in mind. If you have feature requests or feedback, please put **\[ledgerlens]** at the very beginning of your email subject and send it to **xinghan.sde@gmail.com**. As a one-person army, I’ll do my best to prioritize and turn LedgerLens into a more perfect product, step by step.
