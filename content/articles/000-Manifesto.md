As someone majored in finance. I have been bookkeeping and tracking my personal finances since college. For more than a decade, I have put serious effort into understanding where my money actually goes.

Yet even for someone like me, breaking down a large department store receipt into meaningful categories is painfully difficult.

Every few hundred-dollar Costco receipt ends up mostly booked as "groceries." I know I am overbooking some categories and underbooking others. But where did I really spend the money? I have no idea. I have tried to break it down manually several times, and I gave up every single time.

Today, we welcome LedgerLens.

LedgerLens is a receipt scanner not built for small business bookkeeping, but for personal finance and household budgeting insights. It tells your story at the item level. It reveals what you actually consume, how your spending shifts over time, and where small habits quietly compound.

All you need to do is scan your grocery receipt.

Over the past few years, rising living expenses have squeezed households across North America, while capital markets have continued to thrive. I have downloaded countless "money-saving" apps. Coupons, deal platforms, loyalty programs. But almost all of them are sell-side tools.

Being sell-side, their incentives are clear. Their goal is to increase basket size, increase frequency, increase spending. You may save on one deal, but in total you often spend more. Now, with AI, algorithms can understand your behavior better than you do.

Market outcomes reflect the balance of power. The sell side has distribution channels, capital, top talent, and centralized data. Individual consumers have very little leverage.

LedgerLens is my attempt to shift that balance.

With today's AI increasingly accurate at small and time-consuming tasks, a buyers' alliance becomes possible. If sellers can organize data and extract insights, so can buyers. If AI can optimize marketing funnels, it can also optimize our decisions.

This is not about extreme frugality. It is about informed choice. About directing your hard-earned dollars with intention. About building an edge for your family and your future self.

Join LedgerLens. Let's reclaim our power. One receipt at a time.
